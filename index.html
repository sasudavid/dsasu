About Me


I am currently a PhD student in the NLPnorth research unit within the Computer Science Department at the IT University of Copenhagen advised by Dr. Natalie Schluter.
My research interest covers the field of Spoken Language Processing in low resource languages.
In this field of research, I have particular interest in the application of speech processing to improve education outcomes in underprivileged communities.
I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Ashesi University.
